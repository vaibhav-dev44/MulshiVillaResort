About Our Resort

Nestled along the serene banks of Mulshi Lake, just a short drive from Pune, Mulshi Villa Resort offers an idyllic escape into nature's embrace. Our cozy villa resort features comfortable accommodation with lush green lawn, and a private swimming pool.

Our elegantly designed villa boast modern amenities including air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and en-suite bathrooms for ultimate comfort.

Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Mulshi Villa Resort is your gateway to rejuvenation with its strategic location that allows you to explore Mulshi without having to travel far.
