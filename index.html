About Our Resort

Nestled along the serene banks of Mulshi Lake, just a short drive from Pune, Mulshi Villa Resort offers an idyllic escape into nature's embrace. Our luxurious villa resort features eco-friendly accommodations with breathtaking lake views, lush greenery, and organic farm-to-table dining.

Our elegantly designed villas boast private balconies overlooking the lake, modern amenities including air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and en-suite bathrooms for ultimate comfort.

Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Mulshi Villa Resort is your gateway to rejuvenation with boating, fishing, yoga sessions, and proximity to the scenic Western Ghats.
